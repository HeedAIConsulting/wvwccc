Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026 · DRAFT — legal review required before launch.

The West Valley · Warner Center Chamber of Commerce respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, email, phone, and business details when you join, register for an event, donate, or contact us. Payment card data is processed securely by our payment provider (AGMS) and is never stored on our servers.

How we use it

To operate the directory, process memberships, events, and donations, send receipts and chamber communications, and respond to your inquiries. We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You may update your member profile or unsubscribe from communications at any time. Contact (818) 347-4737 or message us with any privacy request.

This is a working draft. Final privacy and terms language must be reviewed by counsel before public launch.
