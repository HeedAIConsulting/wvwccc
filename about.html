The Chamber · Since 1930

Your connection to the
Greater West Valley

For nearly a century, the West Valley · Warner Center Chamber of Commerce has been the voice and the connector of business in the Valley — from Tarzana and Woodland Hills to Reseda, Canoga Park, West Hills, and the Warner Center.

Our mission

Championing local business and community

We connect members to customers, to each other, and to the community — through a trusted directory, year-round events, advocacy, and programs that keep the Valley thriving.

From monthly networking breakfasts and ribbon cuttings to our signature Gala, the Food & Wine event, and Grateful Hearts first-responder fundraisers, the Chamber turns connection into opportunity.
