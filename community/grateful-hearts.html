Honoring our first responders

A heartfelt thank-you to the local LAPD & LAFD heroes who keep our community safe — celebrated with a free Fall Family Fair for the whole West Valley.

About Grateful Hearts

Grateful Hearts is the Chamber's annual community tradition recognizing the dedication and sacrifice of our local police officers and firefighters. The day brings together members, families, and neighbors for a Fall Family Fair — food, music, activities for kids, and a chance to say "thank you" in person to the men and women who serve our community.
