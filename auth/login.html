Need help signing in?
  • Use the email you registered with the Chamber. That's usually your business email from when you joined. If sign-in fails, a different email is the most common reason — call us and we'll look up which one is on file.
  • Lead a group or network? You may have a second sign-in with a different email just for managing your group. Make sure you're using the right one for what you're trying to do.
  • Didn't get the email link? Check your spam or junk folder. Links expire quickly (about 20–30 minutes) — if yours expired, just request a new one.
  • Forgot your password? No need to remember it — use “Email me a link” above and you're in, no password required.
  • Tired of signing in? Leave “Keep me signed in” checked and this computer stays signed in for 30 days.
  • Still stuck? Call the Chamber office at (818) 347-4737.
