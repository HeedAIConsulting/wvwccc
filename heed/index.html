About Michael Bowers

One firm, three directives

The name Heed comes from my days in risk management — where the whole job was to heed the warning: see the exposure before it became a loss, and act on it while there was still time. That instinct never left me, and it became the firm I built.

Risk management taught me that the businesses I cared about never had just one kind of problem. The warning signs showed up everywhere at once — in the technology, in the operations, in the numbers — and the companies that won were the ones that stopped treating them as separate. So I built one firm to read all three signals and answer them together.

For years I've worked shoulder-to-shoulder with companies of every size — from first-location small businesses to established organizations modernizing how they run. I've built the websites and the automations, untangled the operations, and helped owners finally understand the numbers behind their decisions. Different hats, but always the same job: catch what's weighing a business down before it costs them, and turn it into something that moves them forward.

That's the thinking behind one firm with three directives. Heed AI Solutions brings the technology and automation. Heed Business Solutions brings the operational muscle and strategy. And Heed CFO Solutions — launching soon — brings the financial leadership that ties it all together. Work with one directive and you get a partner who can already see the whole picture, not a vendor who only knows their corner of it.

People engage Heed because they want a relationship, not a transaction — someone who heeds the warning on their behalf, stays accountable to the outcome, and treats their business like it's his own. That's the standard I built this firm on, and it's the one I bring to every company I'm fortunate enough to serve.

— Michael Bowers Founder · Heed Consulting Group
