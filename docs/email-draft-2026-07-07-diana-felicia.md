## How to add a member one at a time

1. Sign in and go to **Admin → Members**.
2. Click **+ New member** at the top.
3. Fill in the business name (the only required field), plus contact name, email, phone, address, category, and join date.
4. Click **Add member**. If you included an email, a login is created for them and they receive a welcome email with a link to set their password and manage their own listing.

## New: adding members in bulk (for the busy months)

For months like June where a whole list comes in at once:

1. Go to **Admin → Members** and click **⬆ Bulk upload (CSV)**.
2. Choose the spreadsheet file — the same monthly export you already get (with COMPANY, FIRSTNAME, LASTNAME, EMAIL, PHONE, ADDRESS, CITY, STATE, ZIP, WEBSITE, JOINDATE columns) works as-is.
3. You will see a preview of everyone in the file. Check it looks right.
4. Decide whether to tick **"Send welcome / set-password emails."** Ticked: every new person gets their account invitation right away. Unticked: everyone is added quietly and you can send invitations later.
5. Click **Import**. You get a line-by-line report of what happened.

Two things worth knowing:

- **It never creates duplicates.** If a company is already in the directory, the upload updates their record and attaches their login instead of adding them twice. You can safely re-upload a list that overlaps with a previous month.
