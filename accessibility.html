Accessibility

Our commitment to accessibility

The West Valley · Warner Center Chamber of Commerce is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Conformance

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and to meet the requirements of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). Our build follows accessible practices: semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, sufficient color contrast, descriptive alt text, and reduced-motion support.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We regularly review the site and welcome feedback that helps us improve.

Contact us

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please call (818) 347-4737 or send us a message so we can help and address the issue.
